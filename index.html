Stuy X Columbia GWC | 12/4/20

Thank you to everyone who came to our first panel of the year! Today we heard from Marina Gemma, Dahlia Goshal and Mary McGreal, who talked about their experiences studying CS and applying CS to other scientific fields. The three of them demoed their own computer science projects, which included modeling atom activity, projecting images of the surface of Mercury, and a website modeling UX/UI design principles about how to dye your hair at home. Marina, Dahlia and Mary also answered many questions about how to get started in computer science and how to make the most of CS opportunities in college. Thank you Marina, Dahlia and Mary for being amazing panelists!
