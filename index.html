About Us

WHO WE ARE: A close community of girls at Stuyvesant High School passionate about computer science. OUR MISSION: Provide a supportive and fun environment to learn computer science and help close the gender gap in computer science.

GWC X Lena Lowenstine | 3/18/25

We had our second guest speaker event with Lena Loewenstine, a computer engineer who specializes in cybersecurity and who was also our GWC facilitator at Stuy for 7 years! She briefly discussed her extensive experience in computer science - notably as an FBI agent - and introduced us to cybersecurity - the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. She explained the skills needed to work in cybersecurity and the numerous job opportunities in the field, from careers as ethical hackers (people who are paid to hack into businesses to test their security) to positions in Secret Services! Leana also introduced us to malware reverse engineering, the process of dissecting and analyzing a malware to see how it works, its purpose, and its impact. Later, we worked on a case study in which we used our malware reverse engineering skills to resolve suspicious logins into a company. We ended the event with a Q&A session.
