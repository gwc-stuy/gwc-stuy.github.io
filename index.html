About Us

WHO WE ARE: A close community of girls at Stuyvesant High School passionate about computer science. OUR MISSION: Provide a supportive environment to learn computer science and help close the gender cap in computer science.

OUR VALUES:

Our leadership board includes ten upperclassmen mentors who are here to support members in and out of meetings. Members are placed into small mentoring groups with these mentors to bond and receive whatever help they might need.

üí´ Skill-building: We want to expose members to a wide variety of topics in computer science and teach them about the endless paths one can take with computer science.
